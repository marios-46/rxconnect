2. AI and Data Analytics in Drug Discovery

Artificial intelligence (AI) has shortened the time it takes to develop a drug from an average of 10 years to less than five. Algorithms now analyze massive datasets to identify promising compounds, predict side effects, and optimize dosage levels before human testing begins.

  • Predictive Modeling: AI can simulate how drugs interact with biological systems.
  • Data Integration: Cloud-based systems allow researchers to share results securely worldwide.
  • Cost Reduction: Automation reduces laboratory expenses and trial redundancy.
